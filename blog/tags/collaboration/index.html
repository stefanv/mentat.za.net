The Philosophy of Code Review

#coding
#collaboration

Recently, a colleague of mine was agitated by a comment I left during a code review. Who could blame them: I had asked them to motivate an implementation choice that still didn’t make sense to me—after numerous rounds of discussion. To their great credit, they were supremely polite about it: Thanks for checking in. Overall, I really appreciate your reviews and feedback. At times, I’ve been feeling frustrated that I’m having to address comments that aren’t relevant.
